Membership Drive

The IEEE MEmbership drive for the year 2019-2020 started on 26th August 2019. IEEE Committee members did the class to class publicity during the breaks across all classes from the first year to the fourth year.IEEE Committee members explained the benefits of IEEE membership to all the students during the publicity.Firebird Bots demonstration was conducted at the registration desk by the IEEE-VIT TechWeb Team. People were enthusiastic to know how the bots were programmed.Vice-Principal of VIT, Prof. Varsha Bhosale took the IEEE-GRSS membership. Other professors also showed interest in taking IEEE Membership.

Technical Talk on AI by Ms.Deepthi Anne

The topics included were :
1. The complexity of the model
2. Training and optimization of the AI model
3. Maintaining accuracy
4. Obtaining data for training of AI

Hands-on workshop on Data Visualization using Python.

The students were helped by the team in the installation of the required software on their laptops. Students without laptops were provided with them. The workshop was conducted by the TechWeb team of IEEE-VIT SB.The first part of the workshop covered the following topics: Introduction & Basics of Python including arrays, strings, and other operators, Data structures in Python (list, tuples, and dictionary). The second session of the workshop focused on the applications in Python which is Data Visualization.Both the sessions were informative and interactive.

IEEE Day Celebration.

IEEE VIT & IEEE VSIT organized a technical quiz on IEEE day.Participants ranged from all years (FE, SE, TE and BE) as well as branches (CMPN/ IT/ ETRX/EXTC/BIOM).

Hands-on workshop on Adobe Photoshop

The workshop was conducted by the Creatives team of IEEE-VIT SB. The workshop began with an introduction to designing and adobe Photoshop. The basics included the description of tools and various options available with each tool.The following session included the application of Photoshop - Image manipulation. An interesting image was recreated by combining multiple photos using powerful tools and techniques of the software. Both the sessions were interesting and interactive.
